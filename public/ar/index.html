عن المكتب

  • نبذة تاريخية

    تأسس مكتب عبدالله باكودح للاستشارات المالية في مدينة الدمام بالمملكة العربية السعودية عام 2002م وحصل على سجل تجاري رقم 2050040961 واتبع سياسات متميزة لخدمة عملائه وامتاز بتنوع الخدمات التي يقدمها وبمهنية عالية جداً وأشخاص ذو خبرات طويلة.

  • رؤيتنا

    أن نكون شركاء مع عملائنا في المملكة العربية السعودية ودول الخليج والشرق الأوسط وأن نقدم لهم الإضافة ونزودهم بخدمات الإستشارات المالية المتنوعة وأي خدمات مالية ومهنية أخرى قد يحتاجون لها بمستويات تفوق توقعاتهم.

  • رسالتنا

    تقديم خدمات الاستشارات المالية والحلول المالية بإتباع أرفع المعايير الأخلاقية في العمل المهني بواسطة فريق عمل يتميز بالثقة والنزاهة ومتواجد عندما يريدونا شركاؤنا فالصديق وقت الضيق.

  • أهدافنا

    1. تحديد نقاط القوة والضعف وفرص المخاطر المحتملة وتقديم الحلول المالية التي تضيف قيمة لعملائنا.
    2. مساعدة عملائنا على تحقيق أهدافهم وتجاوز أي صعوبات قد تواجههم.
    3. المحافظة على ارتفاع كفاءة فريق العمل والذي يتمتع بالدقة والنزاهة عن طريق الدورات التدريبية وورش العمل.
    4. اعتماد سياسة أن العملاء شركاء لنا في النجاح، نجاحهم من نجاحنا.
    5. التطوير المستمر في طريقة أداء عملنا والعمل على تقديم النصائح المستمرة لعملائنا لتطوير أداء عملهم أيضاً.
    6. التواصل مع الجمعيات المهنية والهيئات العالمية في مجال عملنا المهني حتى نواكب أحدث متغيرات العصر.

  • مكاتبنا

    تقع مكاتبنا في كل من مدينة الدمام ومدينة جدة ويقوم على إدارة هذه المكاتب خبراء مهنيون على مستوى عالٍ جداً من الخبرة العلمية والعملية ويتمتعون بمستوى عالٍ من الكفاءة والأمانة والنزاهة والدقة في العمل بدعم عدد كبير من الخبراء الأوروبيين من العضو العالمي مجموعة نيكسيا الدولية.

OUR SERVICES

الأنظمة المالية والإدارية

وضع وتطوير الأنظمة المالية والإدارية وذلك بناءً على دراسة منظمة لواقع نشاط العميل ومن ثم اقتراح أفضل الأنظمة الملائمة لنشاط العميل وأيضاً تقديم خدمات تطوير أنظمة الحاسب الآلي للعملاء بناءً على دراسة تفصيلية لاحتياجات العميل ومن ثم تقديم أنسب الحلول الملائمة لوضع العميل ونشاطه التجاري كما نقدم خدمات تقييم الأنظمة المالية والإدارية ومعالجة أي قصور فيها.

Business Valuation

The assessments of companies are increasing in the developed business environment, which resulted from several reasons, including merging with other companies and changing the structure the family businesses operate in especially entry and exit of partners. All of these processes require evaluations based on companies estimated market value using evaluation methods which brings importance and value of the services provided by our professional team to choose the most appropriate evaluation method.

Feasibility Studies

The supervision of the development of the feasibility studies for potential projects upon which decisions by the management can be made. It is worth to mention that the governmental interest in small and medium entities in addition to the need for the facilities to finance the operations have increased the market of feasibility studies.

Preparation and Development of Administrative Regulations

The company develops financial and administrative systems in accordance with the organizational structure of the companies with identifying accounting cycle (such as purchasing cycle for the creditors and the sales cycle and the receivables) and management system in addition to the work of identifying the entire functions and regulations governing companies, as well as the power of each administrative level under the regulatory system integrated.

Strategic Plans and Performance Evaluations

The company develops (long-term and short-term) strategic plans, to suit companies in line with the nature of its activities and achieve its desired objectives and pre-set by the board of directors, as well as assessing these plans through the stages of implementation and identify the causes of deviations and work rectifications, as they are evaluating the performance of employees and to clarify the methods of raising competence productivity.

Change the Corporate Entity

Changing the structure of companies has become a very common trend, especially the family businesses, that changes from a limited liability companies into a closed joint stock companies, this change requires financial, administrative and legal actions. Our company was one of the first companies that pioneered in this area, which includes, the establishment of new companies as it is considered to be related to this activity.

Zakat and Tax Consultancy

Zakat, tax services and submitting zakat deceleration form on behalf of our clients. This includes communications with Zakat and Income Tax Directorate as well as providing advice with regard to Zakat and tax in accordance with the prevailing laws and regulations in this area.

Value Added Tax (VAT)

VAT is a sales tax to be paid on purchase. Currently there is no VAT in Saudi Arabia, however it is expected to be implemented in January 2018, and our team started to prepare for this big change, and have gained knowledge about this tax in similar countries by taking the experience of our global network. Our office will be ready to offer this kind of service when its approved and applied.

Risk Assessment and Management

Providing financial advisory services in the area of financial management for small potential clients who do not have the ability to use financial principles to improve the financial situation towards financiers and suppliers.

Due Diligence

The assessment of due diligence is the aspect of the evaluation of companies and has been described as a separate service because of its importance in the corporate merging and its inclusiveness of all aspects of the company and not the financial aspect only.

Providing Financial Solutions

Providing financial advisory services in the area of financial management for small potential clients who do not have the ability to use financial principles to improve the financial situation towards financiers and suppliers.

Corporate Governance

Provide a unique service that help companies to implement corporate governance and identify irregularities and work to provide advice for companies to address these violations and to work towards achieving full governance, as the government new laws had enforced shareholding companies to declare, in the Board of Directors report all the items that their company did not abide and the reasons for non-compliance.
